About Services

What web technologies do you use? expand_more
We specialize in a high-performance modern stack including React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and TypeScript for the frontend. For backend solutions, we leverage Node.js, PostgreSQL, and specialized headless CMS platforms to ensure maximum scalability and speed.
Do you provide maintenance? expand_more
Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, performance monitoring, and content updates. Our goal is to ensure your digital asset continues to perform at its peak long after the initial launch.

Technical

How do you handle security? expand_more
Security is baked into our development lifecycle. We implement SSL/TLS encryption, secure API authentication protocols, regular dependency audits, and follow OWASP best practices to protect your data and user privacy.
What is your stack for backend development? expand_more
Our primary backend stack consists of Node.js with Express or Fastify, paired with PostgreSQL for relational data or MongoDB for flexible schemas. We also utilize Redis for caching and Docker for containerized deployment environments.

General Inquiry

How can I schedule a consultation? expand_more
You can schedule a consultation by clicking the "Hire Me" button in the navigation or visiting our contact page. We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours to set up an initial discovery call.
What is the typical project timeline? expand_more
A standard high-end portfolio or business site typically takes 4–6 weeks from discovery to launch. Complex web applications with bespoke features may range from 8–12 weeks. We provide a detailed milestone roadmap for every project.
